Overkill - Valorant

The Overkill is all about gaming. It is an e-game competition with a famous game name Valorant. It is An online multiplayer shooting game . There are various maps available like Bind, HAvEn, Split, etc . Each team’s roster will be allowed to have 5 main players and 1 substitute. It is a 5v5 match . A player is allowed to represent only one Team.

Rules

  1. Each team’s roster must consist of at least 5 players.
  2. Each team’s roster will be allowed to have 5 main players and 1 substitute. Once the tournament starts, you cannot replace any player.
  3. A substitute player may only be added before the Tournament Start
  4. This player cannot be part of any other team’s roster which is participating or has participated in the same tournament.
  5. Match must be played in the following format: 5v5.
  6. A player is allowed to represent only one Team.
  7. Solo Players can Register to random teams, but team filling is subject to availability and not guaranteed.
  8. In case of an unprecedented number of registrations, the game mode may be changed to SwiftPlay until Semi-Finals
  9. It is recommended to bring your own laptops and peripherals
