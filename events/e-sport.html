Mobile MayHem - Handled Esports

The main focus of this event is to bring light to the growing esports community in India. This event mainly consists of 4 games (mobile & console combined). The games are put in a tournament format bringing the competitive spirit of players from across the state into one place. There are both individual and team games.
The event consists of 4 games as followed:

1. BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India)
2. CODm (Call of Duty Mobile)
3. Urban Reign 2
4. WWE Smackdown


Game Format

1. BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India)

The tournament will be a 2-round tournament. 1st round will have 5 matches of 15 squads each and the top 3 squads will be qualified for the finale. After 5 matches in the 1st round, the Finale will have 15 teams that will compete for the grand prize pool, and the squad to win the finale will be rewarded with the prize money.

*If the slots of 15 are not filled in every match, then the 1st round matches will be held with the number of occupied slots, and still the top 3 squads will move to the finale.


2. CODm (Call of Duty Mobile)

The tournament format for CODm will be declared on-site, given the number of registrations available, keeping in mind the lesser popularity of the game.



3. Urban Reign 2

This Tournament will consist of 16 matches of 4-player games in the 1st round and the winner of each match will move into the Semi-finals. The 2nd round will be the semi-final round which will consist of 4 matches that will give 1 player each for the Grand Finale. The 3rd round will be the Grand Finale where the final 4 will compete for the prize pool.

*If the slots of 4 are not filled in any of the matches, then the matches will continue with the given number of participants


4. WWE Smackdown

Based of Classic Rules
