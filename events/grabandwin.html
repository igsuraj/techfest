Grab and Win

The Grab and Win is a non-Technical event. It is very fun game In which 6 teams are going to play with 4 members each. This event consists of various games SUCH AS BOOND BOOND SE SAGAR BANTA HAI... This game has basically 7 TO 8 ROUNDS. it will be the type of sports event Between all the technical events. IT is a kind of refreshing event.


Rules

  1. The first team who rings the buzzer, will get the chance to answer.
  2. If the answer is wrong then they will get minus points, if correct they will get a chance to play.
  3. If they completes, he will get points, And if they don't, they will get zero points.
  4. The winners will be drawn according to the points table, the one who remains at the top will declared as winner.

Terms and Conditions

If there is any kind of damage to any equipment, then the participant will be responsible for it and will compensate for it.
